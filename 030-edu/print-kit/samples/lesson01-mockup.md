## Story

There are two dragons:

- The **Taking Dragon** 🐉🔥
Hungry, noisy, always wanting more. It takes silence, time, and peace.

- The **Guardian Dragon** 🐉🌱
Gentle, kind, protecting the forest. It warms without burning, guides without
shouting.

Our choice shapes the world.
